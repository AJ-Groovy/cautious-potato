FEATURED DEALS ON LONGBOARDS

The Longboard Store is a family owned and operated business that was established in 2006. Our early core values that still rein true today is to provide the best quality longboards and accessories at value pricing and delivering a promise of consistent exceptional customer service to each of our valued customers. We sell all longboards with the manufacturer's recommended components. This ensures your longboard will perform with the performance, safety and dependability as engineered by the manufacturer. If customization is your goal, we offer a wide variety of longboard decks, trucks, wheels, and other accessories. Check out the popular brands we sell like Moonshine, Landyachtz, and Stella. By shopping with us, you’re guaranteed free USA shipping and no sales tax. Start browsing our high quality selection today and find the longboards that perfectly matches your riding style.
